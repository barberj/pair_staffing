Dr. Candice Norris-Brown, LPC, NCC, is licensed by the state of Georgia as a Professional Counselor. Dr. Norris-Brown holds a Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Virginia State University, a Masters of Science Degree in Clinical Psychology from Virginia State University, Educational Specialist Degree in School and Guidance Counseling from the University of Georgia and a Doctorate of Philosophy in Counseling and Student Personnel Services from the University of Georgia. I am a compassionate counselor who focuses on individual strengths to build and foster healthy communication and relationships. As a therapist, I am committed to helping empower you to better understand yourself and your behavior. With my encouragement and support we will work together to develop a more effective way of living a positive and productive life.

I use Cognitive Behavior therapy and Solution Focused-Brief therapy treatment methods to help you lead a more fulfilling life. I welcome the opportunity to help you on your journey.
